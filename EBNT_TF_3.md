### Signal Pattern

38 kHz modulation. A simple repeating pattern based on multiples of roughly ~500 μs, with on times in multiples of 10, 2, 1, 1, 1, 1, 2, 1, 1.

E.g. follow pattern confirmed as working.

* 1010 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 1002 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 501 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 501 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 501 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 501 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 1002 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 501 μs on
* 504 μs off
* 501 μs on
